# Cranberry Sauce
{: .no_toc }

---

## Ingredients
<ul>
	<li>Cranberries</li>
	<li>Sugar</li>
	<li>Orange juice</li>
</ul>

## Instructions
Mix everything together in a pot and cook until berries burst.

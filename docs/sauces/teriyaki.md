# Teriyaki Marinade
{: .no_toc }

---

## Ingredients
<ul>
	<li>½ c. soy sauce</li>
	<li>2 tbs. sugar</li>
	<li>1 clove garlic, crushed</li>
	<li>1 small piece ginger, crushed</li>
</ul>

## Instructions
Combine all. Marinate pork or chicken for ½ hour. Pan fry to cook (or broil).

### Cuisine: Asian, Japanese
